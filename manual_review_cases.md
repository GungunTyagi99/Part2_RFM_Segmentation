
# Manual Review Cases

## Case 1: High Spend, Low Recency

Customer Characteristics:
- High monetary value
- No recent purchases

Action:
- Customer success outreach
- Personalized retention offer

## Case 2: Frequent Returns

Customer Characteristics:
- High return rate
- Moderate purchase frequency

Action:
- Investigate product satisfaction
- Improve customer experience

## Case 3: Multiple Negative Support Tickets

Customer Characteristics:
- High ticket count
- Negative ticket outcomes

Action:
- Escalate to support team
- Provide resolution follow-up

## Case 4: Dormant High Value Customer

Customer Characteristics:
- Historically high spending
- No recent engagement

Action:
- VIP win-back campaign
- Exclusive incentives

## Case 5: Sudden Engagement Drop

Customer Characteristics:
- Previously active
- Sharp decline in sessions and visits

Action:
- Trigger re-engagement campaign
- Personalized recommendations
