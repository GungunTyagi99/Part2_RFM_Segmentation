
# Customer Retention Strategy

## Champions
These customers have purchased recently, spend more, and engage frequently.

Action:
- Early access to new products
- VIP rewards
- Referral programs

## Loyal Customers
These customers purchase consistently and are engaged.

Action:
- Loyalty points
- Personalized recommendations
- Exclusive discounts

## Potential Loyalists
These customers are showing positive engagement but are not fully loyal yet.

Action:
- Welcome campaigns
- Category recommendations
- Limited-time offers

## Big Spenders
These customers contribute high revenue.

Action:
- Premium membership
- Personalized support
- Exclusive product bundles

## At Risk
These customers have reduced activity and may churn.

Action:
- Re-engagement emails
- Special discounts
- Customer success outreach

## Dormant Customers
These customers have not interacted recently.

Action:
- Win-back campaigns
- Strong promotional offers
- Feedback surveys
